About Delhi

Delhi, the heart of India, is a vibrant metropolis with a rich history spanning over 5,000 years. From the majestic Red Fort to the modern Lotus Temple, Delhi offers a unique blend of ancient heritage and contemporary culture.

The city is famous for its diverse cuisine, bustling markets, and numerous historical monuments that showcase India's glorious past.

Popular Attractions

Red Fort

A historic fort built in 1638 that served as the main residence of Mughal emperors. It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
